Why typed-claude-hooks?

See what changes when you stop writing hooks without Type-Safety or as bash scripts.

Type-safe everything

Raw hooks give you unstructured JSON piped through stdin. Typo in a field name? Silent bug. typed-claude-hooks gives you real TypeScript types for every event — mistakes are caught at compile time, not production.
